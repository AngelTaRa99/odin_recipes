Lasagna's recipe

This is my favorite food of all time, a mix of cheese and meat that you will love, enjoy one of the best and and most famous italian dishes of the world

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato product
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat souce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the next instructions:

    How to layer Lasagna

    Follow the next order of ingredients.

    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Riccotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with te remaining Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest some minutes before serving
