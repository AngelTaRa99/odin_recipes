Burger's recipe

This famous dish across all USA and the world is one of the best dishes is easy and tasty, keep reading to learn how to do it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Burger bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lighty oil grate.

  2. Step 2

    Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.

  3. Step 3

    Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.

  4. Step 4

    Form into four 3/4-inch thick patties.

  5. Step 5

    Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 70 degrees C

  6. Step 6

    Serve hot and enjoy!
