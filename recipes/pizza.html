Pizza's recipe

This is one of the most common and delicious italian food in the world, a very tasty combination of cheeses, tomato sauce and bread.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 230 degrees C, and lightly grease a pizza pan

  2. Step 2

    Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and suggar. Mix and let stand until creamy. (About 10 minutes)

  3. Step 3

    Add flour, oil and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth.

  4. Step 4

    Let rest for 5 minutes

  5. Step 5

    Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle

  6. Step 6

    Transer to the prepared pizza pan.

  7. Step 7

    Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.

  8. Step 8

    Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving
